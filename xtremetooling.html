My first adventure started with Xtreme Consulting. We were contracted by Microsoft’s marketing organization to deliver hundreds of exhibition-ready Surface laptops. To ready a device, it needs an account, documents, settings, apps, and more. I was the key factor in fully automating this deployment process from a partially manual one. I identified how to obtain the Windows Store Apps installation files back when it wasn’t available, and created PowerShell scripts to automate installing it. We successfully delivered "Experience Manager", which automatically readies any device based on a selected experience choice, such as Government, Healthcare, Commercial, and Education.
